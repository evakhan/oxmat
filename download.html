About Us

Officextracts is an IT distribution company in Bangladesh.

Formed in 2008, the company is the sole distributor for Kaspersky Lab in Bangladesh and Bhutan and specializes in the distribution business of IT Security components and solutions. An ISO 9001:2008 certified company, Officextracts ranks among the top most successful distribution company in the Bangladesh IT sector.

With an absolute conviction to channel business, Officextracts is the only IT distribution company in Bangladesh that does not sell direct to end-customers, but operates in-sync with more than 800 listed channel partners across the country. The company maintains the most professional tech support center with a team of certified, skilled and professional tech professionals to support the channel partners and end-customers alike. Officextracts is acknowledged for it's successful management of Kaspersky Lab in Bangladesh, and in the process pioneering a new market trend for paid software in the country along with a strong awareness for cyber security and anti-piracy.

Officextracts has been awarded by Kaspersky Lab APAC as the "Distributor of the Year" in 2010, the "Most Outstanding Distributor in the Corporate Sector" in 2011 and acknowledged for "Outstanding Contributions" in 2012. In February 2013, The Embassy of the Russian Federation in Bangladesh has awarded Officextracts for it's outstanding contribution in the digital sector and promoting a Russian brand in the Bangladesh IT market.
